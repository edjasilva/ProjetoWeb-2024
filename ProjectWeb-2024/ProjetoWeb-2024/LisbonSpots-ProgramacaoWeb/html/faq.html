Frequently Asked Questions

Question 1: What is LisbonSpots?

"Lisbon Spots" is a website designed to help photography enthusiasts and tourists easily discover the best photographic locations in Lisbon, addressing the common challenge of finding ideal spots for memorable photos during their stay.

Question 2: How can I sign up?

You can sign up by clicking the option of 'Sign up' in the menu or by clicking on the 'Login' option in the naviagtion bar, where you would find the option to sign up.

Question 3: Can I submit my own spots? How?

Yes, you can submit your own spots. To submit your spots, you must be registered into the website and logged in. You can submit spots by navigating to the footer of any window where you'd find the option of 'Blog' or simply by clicking the 'Blog' option in the navigation barof any window.

Question 4: How do I contact customer support?

You can find the 'Support' option in footer of each window , in case you need to contact the costumer support. You would find emails and additional information of developers.

Question 5: Is LisbonSpots free to use?

Lisbon is completely free to use. You don't need to sign up to navigate the website.In case of participation you need to sign up.
